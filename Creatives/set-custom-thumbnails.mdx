Custom thumbnails control the preview image for video creatives before launch. You can choose a frame from the video or upload a separate thumbnail image.

## Step-by-step guide

1. Open the **Creatives** tab.

<img
  src="/images/Custom_thumbnail_1.png"
  alt="Creatives tab showing the creative library"
  title="Creatives tab"
  style={{ width:"100%" }}
/>

2. Select the videos you want to update thumbnails for.

<img
  src="/images/Custom_thumbnail_2.png"
  alt="Selected video creatives in the creative library"
  title="Selected video creatives"
  style={{ width:"100%" }}
/>

3. Click the orange thumbnail icon in the top-right corner.

<img
  src="/images/Custom_thumbnail_3.png"
  alt="Thumbnail action icon in the creative library toolbar"
  title="Thumbnail action icon"
  style={{ width:"100%" }}
/>

4. Choose a specific frame from the video or upload a new thumbnail image.

<img
  src="/images/Custom_thumbnail_4.png"
  alt="Custom thumbnail options for choosing a video frame or uploading an image"
  title="Custom thumbnail options"
  style={{ width:"100%" }}
/>

5. Click **Confirm** to update the thumbnails.

<img
  src="/images/Custom_thumbnail_5.png"
  alt="Confirm button for saving the selected custom thumbnail"
  title="Confirm custom thumbnail"
  style={{ width:"100%" }}
/>

## Thumbnail options

You can set a custom thumbnail in two ways:

1. Choose a frame from the video.
2. Upload a separate thumbnail image.

## Recommendation

Use a clear, representative thumbnail so your team can recognize the video quickly in the creative library and launch flow. If the first frame already represents the video well, use it as the thumbnail to avoid uploading a separate image for each video.

Creatives can be uploaded from several places in Adtool. Once uploaded, they are saved in your creative library so you can organize them in the Content tab and reuse them when building launches.

## Where you can upload creatives

### Upload tab

Use the Upload tab when you want to add new creatives to an ad account before organizing or launching them.

1. Open the **Upload** tab.
2. Upload files or folders by browsing from your computer, or drag and drop them into the upload area.
3. To upload from Google Drive, connect your Google account and select the creatives you want to import.
4. After upload, the creatives are available in your creative library.

### Content tab

Use the Content tab when you are already working in the creative library and want to add more creatives to the selected ad account.

1. Open the **Content** tab.
2. Choose the upload option in the creative library.
3. Upload files or folders from your computer, drag and drop them, or import creatives from Google Drive.
4. Use the Content tab to review, tag, and organize the uploaded creatives before launch.

## Launch creative selection

Use launch creative selection when you are building a launch and need to add new creatives without leaving the launch flow.

1. Start or open a launch.
2. Go to the creative selection step.
3. Upload files or folders from your computer, drag and drop them, or import creatives from Google Drive.
4. Select the uploaded creatives for the launch.
5. The uploaded creatives are also saved to the creative library for future use.

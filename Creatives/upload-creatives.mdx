Creatives can be uploaded from several places in Adtool. Once uploaded, they are saved in your creative library so you can organize them in the Content tab and reuse them when building launches.

## Where you can upload creatives

### Upload tab

Use the Upload tab when you want to add new creatives to an ad account before organizing or launching them.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Upload tab">
    Open the **Upload** tab for the selected ad account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose how to upload">
    Upload files or folders from your computer, drag and drop them into the upload area, or connect Google Drive and select the creatives you want to import.

    <img
      src="/images/upload_creatives1.png"
      alt="Upload tab with options to upload creatives from files, folders, or Google Drive"
      title="Upload tab"
      style={{ width:"100%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the creatives">
    After upload, the creatives are available in your creative library.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Content tab

Use the Content tab when you are already working in the creative library and want to add more creatives to the selected ad account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Content tab">
    Open the **Content** tab for the selected ad account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the upload flow">
    Choose the upload option in the creative library.

    <img
      src="/images/upload_creatives2.png"
      alt="Content tab showing the creative library"
      title="Content tab creative library"
      style={{ width:"100%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add files or import from Google Drive">
    Upload files or folders from your computer, drag and drop them, or import creatives from Google Drive.

    <img
      src="/images/upload_creatives3.png"
      alt="Content tab upload flow for adding creatives"
      title="Content tab upload flow"
      style={{ width:"100%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize the uploaded creatives">
    Use the Content tab to review, tag, and organize the uploaded creatives before launch.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Launch creative selection

Use launch creative selection when you are building a launch and need to add new creatives without leaving the launch flow.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the creative selection step">
    Start or open a launch, then go to the creative selection step.

    <img
      src="/images/upload_creatives4.png"
      alt="Launch creative selection step"
      title="Launch creative selection"
      style={{ width:"100%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload new creatives">
    Upload files or folders from your computer, drag and drop them, or import creatives from Google Drive.

    <img
      src="/images/upload_creatives5.png"
      alt="Upload options inside the launch creative selection flow"
      title="Launch upload options"
      style={{ width:"100%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the creatives for launch">
    Select the uploaded creatives for the launch. The uploaded creatives are also saved to the creative library for future use.

    <img
      src="/images/upload_creatives5(1).png"
      alt="Uploaded creatives ready to select for a launch"
      title="Uploaded creatives in launch selection"
      style={{ width:"100%" }}
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

### **Recommendation**

You can use tagging in many different ways. Down below, I've summed up different ways creative tagging is being used today:

1. Targeted country \
   (ex. DK, US, GB, NO, SE, etc.)
2. Product/service
3. Product-/service-group
4. Batch number \
   (ex. batch #4)
5. Creator

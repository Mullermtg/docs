Use creative tags to organize your creative library so you and your team can quickly find the right assets by market, product, batch, creator, or campaign type. Once a tag is applied, you can use it to keep related creatives grouped and easier to find in your creative library.

## Step-by-step guide

## Recommended tagging system

Use tags that make your creative library easier to filter and compare later. As a rule of thumb, tag anything that will help you find, group, or reuse the creative again later.

Common tagging categories include:

- Market or country, such as DK, US, GB, NO, or SE
- Product, collection or service
- Product or service group
- Batch number, such as batch #4
- Creator

Use creative tags to organize the creative library so your team can find the right assets by market, product, batch, creator, or campaign type. Once a tag is applied, you can use it to keep related creatives grouped and easier to find.

## Step-by-step guide

1. Open the **Creatives** tab.

  src="/images/Custom_thumbnail_1.png"
  alt="Creatives tab showing the creative library"

  style={{ width:"100%" }}
/>

2. Select the creatives you want to tag.

<img
  src="/images/Custom_thumbnail_2.png"
  alt="Selected creatives in the creative library"
  title="Selected creatives"
  style={{ width:"100%" }}
/>

3. Click **Add Tag** in the top-right corner.

<img
  src="/images/Add_tag_3.png"
  alt="Add Tag button in the creative library toolbar"
  title="Add Tag button"
  style={{ width:"100%" }}
/>

4. Choose an existing tag or create a new tag for the selected creatives.

<img
  src="/images/Add_tag_4.png"
  alt="Tag selection menu for choosing or creating a creative tag"
  title="Tag selection menu"
  style={{ width:"100%" }}
/>

5. Click the tag to apply it to the selected creatives.

<img
  src="/images/Add_tag_5.png"
  alt="Selected tag applied to creatives in the creative library"
  title="Applied creative tag"
  style={{ width:"100%" }}
/>

## Recommended tagging system

As a rule of thumb, tag anything that will help you find, group, or reuse the creative again later.

Useful creative tags often include:

1. Market, such as `DK`, `US`, `GB`, `NO`, or `SE`.
2. Product or service.
3. Product or service group.
4. Batch number, such as `batch #4`.
5. Creator.
6. Campaign type.

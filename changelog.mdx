Follow product updates, workflow improvements, and new launch features in Adtool.

<Update label="June 11, 2026" tags={["Launch","Review"]} description="Clearer launch review before publishing">
  ## Clearer launch review before publishing

  Launch review now makes it easier to see whether your ads are ready before you publish.

  You can review creative setup, destination details, and missing requirements in one place, so campaigns are less likely to get blocked at the final step.
</Update>

<Update label="June 10, 2026" tags={["Launch","Meta"]} description="Smart links for missing Meta assets">
  ## Smart links for missing Meta assets

  When a Product Set or Instant Form is missing, Adtool now links you directly to the right place in Meta.

  After creating or updating the asset in Meta, return to Adtool, refresh the list, and continue your launch with less back-and-forth.
</Update>

<Update label="June 6, 2026" tags={["Creatives","Workflow"]} description="Faster creative organization">
  ## Faster creative organization

  Creative organization is now easier when preparing launch batches.

  Use clearer grouping and tags to keep creative variations together, reduce manual sorting, and move from upload to launch faster.
</Update>

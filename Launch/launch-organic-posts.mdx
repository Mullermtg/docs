Use organic posts when you want to launch existing social posts as ads from Adtool. After you select the organic posts, they are added to the launch batch so you can review the batch, add manually created ads if needed, and launch through the selected ad account structure.

## When to use organic posts

Use organic posts when you want to boost an existing post, keep the original organic creative and social post, or launch that post through the ad account structure you are already setting up in Adtool.

## What to know before selecting posts

| **Area** | **What it means** |
| --- | --- |
| Organic Posts tab | Organic posts are selected from the `Organic Posts` tab inside `Select Creatives`. |
| Filters | You can filter organic posts by post type, date, and page. |
| Page selection | The organic posts shown depend on the page selected for the batch. |
| Adding to batch | Selected organic posts are added to the batch after you click `Continue`. |

<Tip>
  If the page you need is not shown in the Organic Posts tab, change the page in `Additional settings` for the batch. Organic posts from that page will then be shown when you select creatives.
</Tip>

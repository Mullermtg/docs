### Step-by-step Walkthrough

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to 'Launch' and click 'Campaign'">
    <img
      src="/images/go_to_launch_select_campaign.png"
      alt="Go To Launch Select Campaign Pn"
      title="Go To Launch Select Campaign Pn"
      style={{ width:"55%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Campaign to dublicate">
    <img
      src="/images/copy_campaign.png"
      alt="Copy Campaign Pn"
      title="Copy Campaign Pn"
      style={{ width:"55%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change the settings you need and click 'Continue'">
    **New Name:** Allows you to change the name of the new campaign.\
    **Edit Budget:** Lets you adjust the budget (only applicable if the campaign uses CBO).\
    **Launch as Active:** Gives you the option to publish the new campaign as either active or inactive.\
    **Ad Sets to Copy:** Lets you choose which ad sets to include in the new campaign.

    <img
      src="/images/copy_campaign_settings(1).png"
      alt="Copy Campaign Settings Pn"
      title="Copy Campaign Settings Pn"
      style={{ width:"55%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust the settings of the Ad Sets and click 'Copy'">
    **New Name**: Change the name of the new ad set\
    **Edit Budget**: Let's you change the budget (if the budget is set to ABO)\
    **Edit Dates**: Gives you the option to change start- & end-date\
    **Launch as Active**: Gives you the option to publish the new ad set active or inactive

    <img
      src="/images/copy_campaign_ad-set-settings.png"
      alt="Copy Campaign Ad Set Settings Pn"
      title="Copy Campaign Ad Set Settings Pn"
      style={{ width:"56%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="You have now succesfully dublicated the Campaign!">
    You can now see your new campaign and ad sets, created with the same settings as the previous one.

    **NOTE**: Weâ€™ve made an exact copy of all settings, including optimization goal, audience, and placement options.\
    The only difference is what you just changed in the previous window.

    This campaign and ad set is fresh and contains no ads - ready for you to launch new ads into.

    <img
      src="/images/copy_campaign_done.png"
      alt="Copy Campaign Done Pn"
      title="Copy Campaign Done Pn"
      style={{ width:"57%" }}

  </Step>

Duplicate a campaign when you want to reuse an existing campaign setup and launch new ads into a fresh campaign structure.

## Duplicate a campaign

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to 'Launch' and click 'Campaign'" titleSize="h4">
    <img
      src="/images/go_to_launch_select_campaign.png"
      alt="Launch page with Campaign selected"
      title="Launch page with Campaign selected"
      style={{ width:"100%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the campaign to duplicate" titleSize="h4">
    <img
      src="/images/copy_campaign.png"
      alt="Campaign copy option in Adtool"
      title="Campaign copy option in Adtool"
      style={{ width:"100%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust the new campaign settings" titleSize="h4">
    You can update:

    - **New Name:** Change the name of the new campaign.
    - **Edit Budget:** Adjust the budget when the campaign uses CBO.
    - **Launch as Active:** Choose whether the new campaign launches as active or inactive.
    - **Ad Sets to Copy:** Choose which ad sets to include in the new campaign.

    <img
      src="/images/copy_campaign_settings(1).png"
      alt="Campaign duplication settings in Adtool"
      title="Campaign duplication settings in Adtool"
      style={{ width:"100%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust the copied ad set settings" titleSize="h4">
    You can update:

    - **New Name:** Change the name of the new ad set.
    - **Edit Budget:** Change the budget when the budget is set to ABO.
    - **Edit Dates:** Change the start date and end date.
    - **Launch as Active:** Choose whether the new ad set launches as active or inactive.

    When the settings are ready, click 'Copy'.

    <img
      src="/images/copy_campaign_ad-set-settings.png"
      alt="Copied ad set settings for a duplicated campaign"
      title="Copied ad set settings for a duplicated campaign"
      style={{ width:"100%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the duplicated campaign" titleSize="h4">
    The new campaign and ad sets are created with the same settings as the original, except for the changes you made during duplication.

    Adtool copies settings such as optimization goal, audience, and placement options. The new campaign and ad sets do not contain ads yet, so they are ready for new ads.

    <img
      src="/images/copy_campaign_done.png"
      alt="Duplicated campaign and ad sets in Adtool"
      title="Duplicated campaign and ad sets in Adtool"
      style={{ width:"100%" }}
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step-by-step Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to 'launch' and create your batch">
    Simple create the batch as you normally would.

    ![Go To Launch](/images/Go_to_launch.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Either launch or save the batch as draft">
    ![Save As Draft 2](/images/Save_as_draft_2.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll down to the batch history and click on the icon in the middle">
    This will copy the link for the previewing the batch

    ![Copy Preview Link](/images/copy_preview_link.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Now send it to the desired person or paste in a new tab">
    This preview will show all the ads from the batch.

    You are able to watch the preview in feed or reel format. Below each ad you'll see a 'show all variations' if you've made multiple text variations.

    ![Copy Preview Link(1)](/images/copy_preview_link\(1\).png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="And now you are all set with your preview link!">
    
  </Step>
</Steps>

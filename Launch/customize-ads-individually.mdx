Use 'Edit Individually' when one or more ads in a batch need their own copy or settings.

## Edit individual ads

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to 'Launch' and click 'Edit Individually'" titleSize="h4">
    <Frame>
      ![Edit Individually button on the Launch page](/images/adjust_single_ads\(1\).png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the ad you want to customize" titleSize="h4">
    Edit the fields for that specific ad.

    When you edit a field, Adtool locks that field for the individual ad. Later batch-level changes, such as updating text for all ads at once, will not overwrite the locked field.

    <Frame>
      ![Individual ad editing view in Adtool](/images/adjust_individually_view.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

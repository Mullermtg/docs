Use 'Schedule ads' when you want the ads in a batch to start or end at a specific time.

## Schedule ads for a batch

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to 'Launch' and open 'Additional Settings'" titleSize="h4">
    ![Additional Settings button on the Launch page](/images/go_to_launch_additional-settings.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Turn on 'Schedule ads'" titleSize="h4">
    Set the start date, end date, or both. Then click 'Confirm Changes'.

    The schedule is applied when you launch the batch.

    ![Schedule ads settings in Additional Settings](/images/schedule_ads.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step-by-step Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to 'Launch' followed by 'Additional Settings'">
    ![Go To Launch](/images/go_to_launch_additional-settings.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle on 'Schedule ads'">
    Now you can set your desired start and/or end date.

    Then just hit confirm, and your scheduling will be planned when you launch the batch.

    ![Schedule Ads](/images/schedule_ads.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

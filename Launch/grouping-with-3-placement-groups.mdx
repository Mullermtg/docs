Adtool supports all three placement groups, meaning you can work with one, two or three formats for the same ad.

**Recommended setup:**

- Vertical: 9x16
- Square: 4x5 or 1x1
- Horizontal: 1x1 or 16x9

**BONUS**: Adtool automatically groups your creatives based on their dimensions. No manual placement setup required.

  <Step title="Next step is to group the creatives for the ads">
    If two or three creatives share the same name - except for the dimension - Adtool will recognize them as belonging to the same group.

    **We support grouping for all 3 main placement groups.**

    **Dimension formats that we currently support:**\
    1x1, 4x5 & 9x16\
    1:1 & 9:16\
    1_1 & 1_1\
    1-1 & 1-1

    **Example:**\
    'Video V6 9x16', 'Video V6 4x5' & 'Video V6 16x9' will automatically be grouped together.

    This allows you to easily launch ads in multiple aspect ratios without any manual setup.

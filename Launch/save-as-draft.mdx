## Save a batch as a draft

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to 'Launch' and create your batch" titleSize="h4">
    When the batch is ready to save, click the arrow shown below.

    ![Save as draft menu on the Launch page](/images/Save_as_draft_1.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Save as draft'" titleSize="h4">
    The batch is saved as a draft and appears in the table below.

    ![Saved draft in the batch history table](/images/Save_as_draft_2.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to the draft when you are ready" titleSize="h4">
    In the batch-uploading history, find the draft and click the pencil icon to edit it again.

    ![Pencil icon for editing a saved draft](/images/Save_as_draft_3.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step-by-step Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to 'launch' and create your batch of ads">
    When you are done, then click on the arrow shown below

    ![Save As Draft 1](/images/Save_as_draft_1.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Save as draft'">
    Now your draft will be visible as a draft in the table below

    ![Save As Draft 2](/images/Save_as_draft_2.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Come back when you are ready to launch">
    In the batch-uploading history, you'll be able to find you draft and edit it again by clicking on the pencil shown below

    ![Save As Draft 3](/images/Save_as_draft_3.png)
  </Step>

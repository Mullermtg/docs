Use flexible ads when you want Meta to combine multiple creative assets and variations inside the same ad. In Adtool, the launch flow starts like other ad types, but the grouping step is where you decide which creatives should belong to the same flexible ad.

# Step-by-step guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Launch" titleSize="h4">
    Go to `Launch` and click `Select Creatives`.

    <Frame>
      ![Launch page with the option to select creatives for a new batch](/images/Go_to_launch_select_creatives.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the creatives for the batch" titleSize="h4">
    Select the creatives you want to include in the flexible ad launch, then click `Continue`.

    <Tip>
      Use filters, `Select all`, or click and drag to select several creatives faster.
    </Tip>

    <iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Nx85-E0xQn0?autoplay=1&mute=1&loop=1&playlist=Nx85-E0xQn0&playsinline=1&controls=0&modestbranding=1&rel=0&disablekb=1&fs=0&iv_load_policy=3" title="Selecting creatives for an Adtool launch" frameBorder="0" className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl pointer-events-none" allow="autoplay; encrypted-media" aria-hidden="true" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Group creatives into the flexible ad" titleSize="h4">
    Drag the creatives that should belong to the same flexible ad into the flexible ad group.

    <Expandable title="How flexible ad grouping works">
      A flexible ad group contains the creative assets Meta can combine for that ad. Keep creatives in the same group when they are meant to be tested together as one flexible ad.

      | **Creative setup** | **How to group it** |
      | --- | --- |
      | One flexible ad | Drag all creatives for that ad into the same flexible group. |
      | Multiple flexible ads in one batch | Keep each flexible ad in its own group. |
      | Creatives that should not be mixed | Keep them in separate groups. |
    </Expandable>

    <iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/bKrjhPSB2aw?autoplay=1&mute=1&loop=1&playlist=bKrjhPSB2aw&playsinline=1&controls=0&modestbranding=1&rel=0&disablekb=1&fs=0&iv_load_policy=3" title="Grouping creatives for a flexible ad" frameBorder="0" className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl pointer-events-none" allow="autoplay; encrypted-media" aria-hidden="true" />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add ad details" titleSize="h4">
    Click `Continue`, then fill out the remaining ad information, including primary text, headline, description, CTA, and any other required launch settings.

    Review the previews before launching so you can confirm the creatives and text look correct together.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch the ads" titleSize="h4">
    When the batch is ready, click `Launch`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

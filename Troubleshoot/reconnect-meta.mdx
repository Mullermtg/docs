title: "Reconnect Meta"
sidebarTitle: "Reconnect Meta"
description: "Refresh Adtool's Meta connection when Facebook Pages, businesses, Instagram accounts, or ad accounts are missing."
---

Use this guide when Adtool is missing a Meta asset you should be able to use, such as a Facebook Page, business, Instagram account, or ad account. Reconnecting Meta refreshes the access Adtool can read from your Meta login.

Reconnecting does not give you new permissions inside Meta. If your Meta user does not have access to an asset in Meta Business Manager, you need to get that access in Meta before Adtool can show it.

## Step-by-step guide

### 1. Open Settings

In Adtool, open `Settings` from the left-hand navigation.

<img
  src="/images/FB_IG_PAGE1.png"
  alt="Adtool Settings page in the left-hand navigation"
  title="Adtool Settings page"
  style={{ width: "100%" }}
/>

### 2. Go to Connections

Open `Connections`, then reconnect the Meta or Facebook integration.

<img
  src="/images/FB_IG_PAGE2.png"
  alt="Adtool Connections page with the Facebook integration reconnect option"
  title="Adtool Connections page"
  style={{ width: "100%" }}
/>

### 3. Choose Edit previous settings

When Meta opens the permission flow, choose `Edit previous settings`. This lets you review which assets Adtool can access.

<img
  src="/images/r.png"
  alt="Meta permission dialog with the Edit previous settings option"
  title="Meta permission dialog"
  style={{ width: "100%" }}
/>

### 4. Grant access to current and future assets

In the Meta permission flow, choose access to all current and future Facebook Pages, businesses, Instagram accounts, and related assets that Adtool should be able to use.

We recommend choosing current and future access. This lets Adtool keep fetching newly added Pages, businesses, Instagram accounts, and related assets without asking you to reconnect every time something new is added in Meta.

If you select assets individually instead, Adtool can only access the specific assets you choose during this permission flow. When new Pages, businesses, Instagram accounts, or related assets are added later, you may need to reconnect Meta again before they appear in Adtool.

<img
  src="/images/opt_in_to_allcurrent_and_future_pages.png"
  alt="Meta permission setting for all current and future Facebook Pages"
  title="Meta permission setting for Facebook Pages"
  style={{ width: "100%" }}
/>

<img
  src="/images/opt_in_to_allcurrent_and_future_businesses.png"
  alt="Meta permission setting for all current and future businesses"
  title="Meta permission setting for businesses"
  style={{ width: "100%" }}
/>

<img
  src="/images/opt_in_to_all_current_and_future_IG_accounts.png"
  alt="Meta permission setting for all current and future Instagram accounts"
  title="Meta permission setting for Instagram accounts"
  style={{ width: "100%" }}
/>

### 5. Return to Adtool and check the missing asset

Go back to Adtool and check the place where the asset was missing. If you were looking for a Facebook Page or Instagram account, also confirm that the correct ad account is selected in `Settings`.

## If the asset still does not appear

Check that your Meta user has the right access in Meta Business Manager for the missing Page, business, Instagram account, ad account, or campaign.

<Info>
  If you have confirmed the Meta access, reconnected Meta, and the asset still does not appear in Adtool, contact support and include:

  - The affected ad account or business
  - The missing Page, Instagram account, ad account, business, or campaign name
  - Confirmation that your Meta user has access to the asset in Meta Business Manager
  - When you reconnected Meta in Adtool
</Info>

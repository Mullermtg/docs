sidebarTitle: "Can't see my ads after successful launch"

Meta can sometimes be slow to load the cache when launching ads. In rare cases, after clicking “Launch,” the ads may show as “Success,” but when you click “Open Ads,” you might not be able to see them.

Below, we’ll show you how to view the ads you just launched:

### Step-by-step guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'open ads' in the batch history overview">
    <img
      src="/images/open_ads.png"
      alt="Open Ads Pn"
      title="Open Ads Pn"
      style={{ width:"55%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Not seeing the ads after launch? - go to 'Ad sets'">
    <img
      src="/images/meta_ads_missing.png"
      alt="Meta Ads Missing Pn"
      title="Meta Ads Missing Pn"
      style={{ width:"55%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click “Edit” on the ad set">
    <img
      src="/images/click_edit_ad_set.png"
      alt="Click Edit Ad Set Pn"
      title="Click Edit Ad Set Pn"
      style={{ width:"55%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Now you are able to see your ads">
    Now you should now be able to see them. When you go back to the overview, you’ll typically notice that they’re also visible there.

    <img
      src="/images/editing_ad_set.png"

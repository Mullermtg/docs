Use this guide when a Facebook Page or Instagram account is missing from Adtool while you are setting up or launching ads. In most cases, the Page or account is either not connected to the selected ad account in Adtool, or Adtool needs its Meta permissions refreshed.

## Check the selected ad account

1. Open `Settings` in Adtool.
2. Select the ad account you are working from in the left-hand list.
3. Confirm that this is the same ad account you want to use for the launch.

<img
  src="/images/FB_IG_PAGE1.png"
  alt="Adtool Settings page where you can select an ad account"
  style={{ width: "100%" }}
/>

## Show and connect missing Pages or Instagram accounts

1. With the correct ad account selected, open the Facebook Page or Instagram account connection area.
2. Select `Show all Facebook pages` if a Facebook Page is missing.
3. Select `Show all Instagram pages` if an Instagram account is missing.
4. Find the missing Page or Instagram account and connect it.

<img
  src="/images/FB_IG_PAGE3.png"
  alt="Adtool ad account settings with options to show all Facebook Pages or Instagram accounts"
  style={{ width: "100%" }}
/>

## If the Page or Instagram account is still missing

If the asset does not appear after checking the selected ad account and showing all available Pages or Instagram accounts, reconnect Meta to refresh the permissions Adtool can read from your Meta login.

Follow the `Reconnect Meta` guide, then return to this page and check the same ad account again.

Reconnecting Meta refreshes Adtool access. It does not grant new permissions inside Meta, so your Meta user still needs access to the missing Page or Instagram account in Meta Business Manager.

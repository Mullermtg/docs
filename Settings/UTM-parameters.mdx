### Step-by-step Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to 'Settings'">
    <img

      alt="FB IG PAGE1 Pn"
      title="FB IG PAGE1 Pn"
      style={{ width:"55%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the ad account for which the UTM parameters need to be changed.">
    <img
      src="/images/Select_ad_accounts.png"
      alt="Select Ad Accounts Pn"
      title="Select Ad Accounts Pn"
      style={{ width:"55%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll down to the section: 'UTM tracking'" iconType="regular">
    <img
      src="/images/select_utm-paramter.png"
      alt="Select Utm Paramter Pn"
      title="Select Utm Paramter Pn"
      style={{ width:"55%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your UTM-parameter">
    **Recommendation:** \
    By checking **“Use for all accounts,”** the setting will be applied to all your accounts, so you don’t have to configure them one by one.

    When you use **“Use for all accounts,”** it will override any customizations already made in other account settings for UTM-parameters.

    <img
      src="/images/select_utm-paramter(1).png"

      title="Select Utm Paramter(1) Pn"
      style={{ width:"55%" }}
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Example:

Below, you can see an example of a UTM parameter.

```
utmsource=facebook&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign={{campaign.name}}&utm_term={{adset.name}}&utm_content={{ad.name}}\_
```

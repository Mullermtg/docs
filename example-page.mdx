---
title: "Smart Links for Product Sets and Instant Forms"
---

Creating missing Product Sets and Instant Forms is now faster from Adtool.

When selecting a Product Set or Instant Form, you can now open the right place in Meta directly from the dropdown. After creating or updating the asset in Meta, return to Adtool and refresh the list to continue your launch.

## What’s New

- Open Meta directly from the Product Set selector.
- Open Meta directly from the Instant Form selector.
- Refresh Product Sets and Instant Forms without leaving the launch flow.
- See how many available Product Sets or Instant Forms are currently loaded.

## Why It Matters

Before, if a Product Set or Instant Form was missing, you had to leave Adtool and manually find the right place in Meta Business Manager.

Now Adtool takes you straight to the relevant Meta page, so you can create or update what you need and continue launching with less back-and-forth.
